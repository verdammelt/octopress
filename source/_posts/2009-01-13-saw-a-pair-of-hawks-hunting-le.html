Local squirrel running scared. published: true date: 2009-01-13 categories: [] posterous_url: http://verdammelt.posterous.com/saw-a-pair-of-hawks-hunting-le posterous_slug: saw-a-pair-of-hawks-hunting-le --- We heard a strange bird call when we stepped out of the house and then

 Then we saw the second hawk - they were a pair. Finally they flew off
